Nivea sun anti-age face sun cream SPF 50+

I recently got this and I do like it, but it is not the best for oily skin.

It is a very thin, very comfortable lotion. It spreads out easily and absorbs pretty quickly. However, it does leave a slight film on the skin.

I love that other products apply so easily over top, but it does make my face shiny after about 4-5 hours.

Of course the SPF50! is fantastic and very beneficial! The price is also great, because a little of this stuff goes a mile.

I just think that I need to venture a little bit more and maybe try the LaRoche Posay Anthelios Dry Touch next - I have heard good things.

If you have dry skin, this product would absolutely work well for you!
